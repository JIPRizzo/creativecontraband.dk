Services

Story-led video for Danish and Scandinavian companies entering or expanding in Spanish-speaking markets. Bilingual production, culturally fluent, built to work on both sides of the room.

About

The person behind the studio

Creative Contraband Productions is the studio of José Rizzo — editor, director, and visual storyteller based in Copenhagen.

I craft story-led video work for brands, founders and projects that have something meaningful to express.

I’m particularly interested in helping Danish and select Scandinavian companies tell their stories in Spanish-speaking markets without losing meaning, nuance, or aesthetic integrity.

Translation is the part we see. Through refined questioning, experimentation and sharpening, we'll refine your story so it lands with the people they’re intended to inspire.
